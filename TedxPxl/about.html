TEDxAmsterdam is organized by a nonprofit foundation (stichting) driven by volunteers and financially supported by sponsors from the corporate world. The main goal of the foundation is to organize several annual TEDxAmsterdam events and create a movement around ‘ideas worth doing’.

TEDxAmsterdam is an initiative of Jim Stolze, Dutch author and internet thinker, who was a speaker at TED 2009. He was granted the right to organize TEDxPxl by the TED organization.

This year TEDxAmsterdam will take place on the 28th of November in the Stadsschouwburg Amsterdam. Find out more information on this website.
